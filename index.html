Who we are?

The GirlUp Campaign was founded by the United Nations Foundation in 2010. The campaign was characterized as urgent, highlighting the need for gender equity and women empowerment, especially for adolescent girls in regressive places. Numerous GirlUp chapters like GirlUp Ludhiana are the heart and soul of this mission. These chapters bring together like-minded, compassionate and hard-working individuals who are eager to see significant change in the world. Every year, GirlUp clubs advocate women's rights urging girls to stand up for themselves and turn into change makers of the future. Because when girls rise, we all rise.
